WHO WE ARE

OUR RESTAURANT WAS BORN OUT OF LOVE FOR FOOD AND A DESIRE TO SHARE THAT PASSION WITH OTHERS. OUR FOUNDER HAS ALWAYS BEEN FASCINATED BY THE VERSATILITY AND NUTRITION FROM FOOD, AND HE WANTED TO CREATE A PARADISE OF A RESTAURANT THAT CELEBRATED THIS INCREDIBLE GIFT!

OUR JOURNEY

Our food story dates back to 1984 when the founder, a passionate food lover, had the urge of serving the most delicious recipes for other foodies like himself. It was then that he met steve, a passionate chef who shared similar interests.

They started a small food let that offered takeaway only. Seeing the love the city had for their delicious recipes, they expanded their team with 8 more chefs from different parts of the country so we could serve more variety on the table.

Ever since, we've been known as the best restaurant in the country serving over 1 lakh customers everyday. Humbled by the love we've received, we strive to continue giving you the best always.
